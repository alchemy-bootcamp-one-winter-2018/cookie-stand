The custom font is Lobster. Add it to a page with this in the HTML head:
<link href="https://fonts.googleapis.com/css?family=Lobster"
rel="stylesheet">

The color palette:
Reddish, rgb(172, 72, 60)
Yellow, rgb(228, 250, 137)
Salmon, rgb(249, 126, 112)
Light blue, rgb(69, 125, 198)
Darker blue, rgb(69, 113, 172)
